Introduction

Mi Portal FONE is an online platform provided by Mexico's Secretaría de Educación Pública (SEP) for teachers and administrative staff. This platform gives users access to essential payroll documents, including pay stubs, payment history, and more, helping to streamline financial management in the public education sector.

Key Features of Mi Portal FONE

Payroll Receipts

Download pay stubs (talones de pago) in PDF format with detailed salary breakdowns.

Payment History

Access years of payment records, helpful for financial tracking and tax purposes.

Secure Access

Uses CURP and password for secure, encrypted login to protect sensitive data.

How to Register for Mi Portal FONE

  1. Visit the official Mi Portal FONE website.
  2. Enter your CURP and email address to begin registration.
  3. Create a secure password and verify your account via email.
  4. Log in using your CURP and password to access your payroll information.

Benefits of Mi Portal FONE

The platform offers a range of benefits, including easy access to payroll information, transparency in salary deductions and bonuses, and a convenient 24/7 online service. By transitioning from paper to digital pay stubs, Mi Portal FONE saves time, reduces errors, and simplifies financial management for educators across Mexico.
